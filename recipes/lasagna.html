Lasagna


Description

Tender sheets of pasta, a creamy ricotta cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce
are all topped with more cheese and baked until browned and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Spices
  • Sauce
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Mozzarella
  • Egg
  • Parsley
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parmesan
  • Onion
  • Lasagna

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake for 1 hour until browned and bubbly.
